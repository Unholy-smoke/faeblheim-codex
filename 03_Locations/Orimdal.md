Dwarven settlement inside the [[Worldspire]], hollowed through the Spire with platforms, rope bridges and exhausting stairs below, then geothermal stone buildings cut into the walls higher up. Nethercite ore is sent upward from here, and [[Kompir]] says the dwarves make armour for local giants in return for housing.

The fighting tournament and poisoned food incidents occurred here while Karl's crew were also present.

**First seen:** Session 37; **Last seen:** Session 38.
