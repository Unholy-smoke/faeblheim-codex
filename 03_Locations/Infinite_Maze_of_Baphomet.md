Hellish maze realm associated with [[Baphomet]]. [[Deacon_Fned]] said the mask of Anghraboddh could be destroyed by casting it into the Great Forge here. The palace garden maze briefly became a foggy, sulphurous version of this place, full of demon dogs and dangerous planar weirdness.

**First seen:** Session 33; **Last seen:** Session 33.
