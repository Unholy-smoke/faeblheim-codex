### Overview
Amon Hur, real-world name Golmund Yi, is a vampire gangster who uses his in-game activities to fund his real-world criminal activities.

He is the head of the [[Carnium_Consortium|Carnium Consortium]], which seeks to expand from the now-ruined [[Sverelt]] into Ym. He is acquiring towns. His last acquisition was made through his aide [[Lee-Kin_Knightly|Kinleigh Knightley]], an elf who paid a green dragon to poison a town's water supply.

The party stopped the dragon and brought life back to the town, but the purchase had already been made. They tasked local retired soldier [[Cronin|Kronid]] to keep an eye on things.
