### Overview
The party is delivering a note to a jeweller concerning a mysterious [[Sun_Pearl|Sun Pearl]]. Lin Larkin was the one who commissioned it. The item appears to have significant metagame implications.

This page absorbs the old Sun Pearl Delivery stub.

**First seen:** Session 12; **Last seen:** Session 12.

### Current open lead
Having befriended [[Serpae]] and the rest of the sahuagin, the party learns of the [[Sun_Pearl|Sun Pearl]] that [[Lin_Larkin|Lin Larkin]] commissioned. Since returning to NPC form, he appears to have dropped this request.

[[Lin_Larkin|Lin Larkin]] appears to be running a smuggling trade between the Island of Havers, under the alias [[Tenris]], and his apartment in [[Vilaine]], [[St_Malia|St Malia]].

Bryce has been promoted to Chief of Pearl Trade. He has yet to do anything.

**Status:** Open.
