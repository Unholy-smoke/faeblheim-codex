### Overview
The party has encountered the vampire Killian Graves, an accountant who was Amon Hur's accountant before the vampire was turned. Hur liked Graves so much that he forced him into servitude by turning him into a vampire at age 92.

Graves hates his life, but cannot kill himself because he is under Amon's control. The party has vowed to destroy him as soon as they can.
