### Overview
The party encountered a bloodwitch aliasing herself as Ms Hainegrove in [[Lataine]], [[St_Malia|St Malia]]. She is a powerful hemalurgist interested in Orm, the Seething Scale: a legendary dagger.

The dagger is currently sitting in evidence in a Lataine jail after an adventurer named Ismius Ordelieu was framed for murder by his evil sister Esmia.
