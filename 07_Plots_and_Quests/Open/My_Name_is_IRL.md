### Overview
At the Armourer's Daughter, the party meets the Griman Questlord Ingram, a man with ties to the real world named IRL.

The party are suspicious of two ravens apparently watching them. IRL tells them not to worry.

IRL asks the party to investigate the sewers, where an influx of rats is ruining his tavern. The party discovers a portal to the Shadowfell. IRL tells them to meet him when they have time.

**Status:** Open.
