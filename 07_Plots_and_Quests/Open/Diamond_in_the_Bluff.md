### Overview
Diamonds are being hoarded by the world's clerics, causing a massive imbalance to the game.

This is due to many of the world's diamond mines collapsing. Mudora, a diamond mine owner with ties to [[Lin_Larkin|Lin Larkin]], has been unable to produce new ones for some time.
