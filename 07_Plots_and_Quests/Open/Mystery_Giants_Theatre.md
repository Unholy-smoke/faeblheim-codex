Investigation into murders among the giants of the [[Worldspire]], beginning in [[Etzba]] after Queen Murgata's murder and [[Regent_Murgato|Regent Murgato]]'s rise. Autopsies show throat slashes and chest or back trauma, while witnesses describe ghostly beings appearing and disappearing.

The culprit threat is linked to the [[Megurash]], including [[Midnakesh]], and escalates as the party climbs through [[Cafregil]], [[Gufragel]] and [[Rosh]].

**Status:** Open.

**First seen:** Session 35; **Last seen:** Session 41.
