## Summary

- The party battles Anghraboddh through two stages: first with a huge bone scythe, then with horns that become daggers.
- Anghraboddh dissolves when killed.
- Ahead, an altar is sucking power from the top of the Spire.
- Dagger is revealed as the mastermind behind the altar and the draining of Taiia's power.
- The party tries to talk him out of ruining the world again, but he wants to be alone in a cleansed Faeblheim.
- Dagger raises the Ceran Palace a hundred feet into the air on a stone platform, like another Worldspire.
- Dagger is too powerful, but Karl's crew arrives and helps the party hold him off. Boss loses an arm to necrotic damage.
- The party survives long enough to crack the altar.
- A Saint spills out and says he is one of the Monarchs, and so is Dagger.
- The party learns that they need to free the other Saints to stand any chance against Dagger.
- One Saint is in Grima, one is elsewhere, and the last two are in the Ceran Palace itself.
- The party levels up and each receives an item that lets them long rest inside two turns.
