## Summary

- The party hears many giant stories and legends from their imp.
- They travel to Cafregil to continue investigating murdered giants.
- Emory, a city guide, helps them navigate the giant city.
- The party visits a giant tavern, then the small-people bar called The Escape.
- In The Escape, a dragonborn and a half-orc fight near a strange portal; the half-orc gets an arm stuck in the portal and it has to be cut off.
- The party meets old friends from the boat, also present for the tournament.
- Bogsū duels the half-orc and wins by a single hit point.
- Bogsū prays at the temple, is dragged into darkness by a psychic killer and nearly dies.
- The others find a kobold murdered in an alley and rush to rescue Bogsū at the temple.
- Emory identifies the staring face in the party's night visions as the Megurash, evil spirits from long ago that now threaten the giants again.

## Source Notes

- Lots of giant stories and legends told by our imp.
- Cafregil is the big giant city. We head there to do more autopsies on murdered giants.
- Reach the city, speak to guards.
- City is a sort of underground warren built above ground. Here as first line of defence for Spire.
- Meet Emory, a city guide.
- We head to the giant tavern first. We feel little.
- Don't learn a lot. Head to small people part of town and into a bar: The Escape.
- Up on the balcony a dragonborn and half-orc are fighting near a weird portal. Orc gets arm stuck in portal and has to have it chopped off.
- Everyone excited for the tournament.
- We meet our old friends from the boat. They are here for the tournament too.
- Me and the half-orc agree to a quick duel to warm up. Bogsū wins by a single hit point. Lots of mutual respect.
- Bogsū goes for a bit of a pray at the temple in town, still pretty knocked up. Kneels and prays. Opens eyes and all is dark. Psychic killer attacks him and down he goes.
- The others find the kobold murdered in an alley, no sign of Bogsū. They race to the temple.
- Rescue Bogsū from near death, gash down his back.
- Turns out the kobold was long dead: five hours ago. Clearly was someone or something else.
- At night we get visions of a staring face. Emory tells us it is the Megurash.
- They are evil spirits from long ago. Threatening the giants again now.
- We prepare to head up the Spire.
