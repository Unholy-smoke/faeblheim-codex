## Summary

- After shopping, the party begins climbing the Spire.
- Stone giant guards suggest that the Tsarina's seal should grant some recognition, but that Murgato will be less respected higher up the Spire.
- The settlement inside the Spire is called Orimdal, a dwarven hollow of platforms, rope bridges and 120,000 steps.
- The party observes the fighting tournament and learns at least one combatant was murdered before competing.
- Karl and his crew are also at the tournament.
- Tournament attendees begin dying from poisoned food.
- After two hours of climbing, the party meets Bobo, the head monkey massager.
- The party must choose whether to keep climbing or use the internal elevator.
