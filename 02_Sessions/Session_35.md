## Summary

- The party nears the last town before the plains and meets the Reinhardts, refugees from Sverelt led by Arigal.
- The Spire's main city has a gambling tournament, but people who go there have not returned.
- Bogsū wins money in an epic game of Liar's Dice.
- The party shops with the brewer and buys another corrupted item.
- Etzba, the town at the base of the Spire, is full of giants.
- The party learns that Queen Murgata has been murdered and Regent Murgato is the new leader.
- The party begins investigating the spate of murders.
- Autopsies show huge slashes to the throat and signs that something burst from the chest or back.
- A small giant child witnessed a murder.
- At a crime scene, Eliyah learns from a lizard that a ghostly thing appeared, disappeared and reappeared.
