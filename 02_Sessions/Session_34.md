## Summary

- The party is summoned to breakfast with the Tsarina and discusses strategy.
- The palace will train peasants into soldiers.
- The Tsarina grants the party her sigil as proof of her authority and sends them to treat with the giants.
- In a private meeting with Bogsū, the Tsarina begins choking on a red feather and becomes frightened that powers can affect reality.
- The party heads north towards the Spire and sends a bird messenger to Bryce's new guild, asking them to watch the door to his house.
- On the way through high mountain country, storms gather around the plains.
- In a town near the Spire, the party meets Friar Blidd, whose companions were murdered by goblins.
- Blidd is somehow compromised. After he bites Kai, the party realises the problem may be lycanthropy.
