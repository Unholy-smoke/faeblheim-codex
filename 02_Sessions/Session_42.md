## Summary

- The party enters the broken storm giant city and splits from Karl's crew.
- They encounter multiple monsters while moving through Rosh.
- Taiia's power is being sapped into an altar at the central palace, preventing Bogsū from properly reaching her.
- The party meets Alvus, a near-dead cloud giant, and heals him enough for him to join them.
- The party kills the resurrected Hunt Master and another giant riding a behir.
- After resting, the party sees Anghraboddh, General of Baphomet, ahead: a huge deer-skull demon.
- The party prepares to enter his maze.
