## Summary

- In flashback, the party speaks to Kamu and Wilde, who are taking Pierot's body away from the Spire but still plan to fight in the tournament.
- Back in the present, the party travels by dwarf-driven wagon through a tiny tunnel to Orimdal.
- Orimdal is a geothermal settlement of stone buildings cut into the walls, rope bridges, lava below and nethercite ore travelling upward.
- Kompir, the Head Smith, explains that the dwarves make armour for local giants in return for housing.
- The party proceeds towards the fire giant citadel of Levragel.
- Outside Levragel, chained dead monsters come to life while fire giants battle other huge creatures.
- The party joins the fight against an undead dragon and hydra.
- Eliyah polymorphs the undead dragon into a snail before being petrified, and Kai saves stone elephant Eliyah from sliding into lava.

## Source Notes

- Flashback to earth giant city two episodes ago. Talking to Kamu and Wilde. They are about to take the body of Pierot away from the Spire but still planning to fight in the tournament.
- Back to the present: in the Orimdal pub.
- Turns out Orimdal is the town higher up the Spire. Nethercite ore from down here gets sent up there.
- Up the wagon through a tiny tunnel for a day. Wagon driven by dwarf, pulled by a mule. Uneventful.
- Arrived in Orimdal. Geothermal, stone buildings cut into the walls.
- Rope bridges over the gap, lava below.
- Kompir is the Head Smith.
- Long chat. They make lots of armour for local giants in return for housing.
- We decide to proceed via the fire giant citadel of Levragel. It will be hot.
- The fire giants have huge magical creatures hanging from chains outside the citadel. Sacrifices or rituals.
- Up to Levragel. It is fucking hot. Looking down is a sea of lava. Huge rocky plateau houses Levragel. Houses made of solidified molten rock, misshapen.
- As we step out, the chained dead monsters have come to life. Various fire giants fighting big monsters.
- We join the fight with an undead dragon. Elia polymorphs it into a snail, just before being petrified.
- The dog drops the snail over the edge. It hits the lava, dies as a snail, turns around to fly back at us.
- Kill the dragon and the hydra. Stone elephant Elia sliding towards edge! Kai dimension doors it to safety.
