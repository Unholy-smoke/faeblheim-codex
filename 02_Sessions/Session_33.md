## Summary

- Bogsū is sad that the Tsarina has not been reading his letters.
- The party visits the Cathedral of Taiia and speaks with Deacon Fned about faith and the mask of Anghraboddh.
- Fned says the mask is immensely powerful and can be destroyed by a Wish spell or by casting it into the Great Forge in the Infinite Maze of Baphomet.
- In the palace maze, the party is drawn into a hellish maze filled with fog, lightning, sulphur and demon dogs.
- A terrified royal soldier, Ahmed, joins the party but is later crushed during the chaos.
- The party meets a strange dark-skinned original player/shopkeeper who wants magic items in exchange for sending them home.
- Alayah's magic beans become ten magical toads, which then become a genie and a string of dangerous creatures, including a balor, a silver dragon, xorn and a cloud giant.
- The shopkeeper uses the genie to remove the monsters and send the party home.
- Back in the centre of the palace maze, the party uncovers an altar and writings about the Second Cataclysm, the God-Felling Star and Taiia's six champions or saints.
- Inside the cracked altar, Eliyah finds another altar with more figures. Blue human hands grab her antennae and heal her, while voices ask for help.
- Deacon Fned identifies the outer figure as Saint Carafect and the trapped inner figures as Saint Corellon and Saint Rillifane.
- The party returns the next day with Fned, Army Commander Ject and soldiers, but learns little more.

## Source Notes

- Start in our rooms. Bogsū sad the Tsarina isn't reading his letters.
- Heading out to the maze again.
- Barracks, training ground, petting zoo, amphitheatre, library.
- Off to the Cathedral of Taiia. Two orderlies tidying the place up after morning service.
- Deacon Fned is in charge. Chat to him about faith.
- Ask him about the mask of Anghraboddh.
- It's very powerful. We should destroy it? Has to go in the fires of hell?
- The Mask of Anghraboddh is our sudden new mission for the future. It can be destroyed with a Wish spell, or in the Infinite Maze of Baphomet by casting it into the Great Forge.
- We head to the maze in the gardens.
- Suddenly the maze gets weird. Fog. Lightning. Sulphur. Suddenly in hell?
- Demon dog attacks, we kill it fast.
- Royal soldier, terrified, appears. Ahmed. Scared and lost. Joins us. I take his spear.
- Kill three more dogs eating the corpse of another soldier.
- Follow blood trail. Meet a strange black-skinned guy in a makeshift tent. He is clearly some sort of original player. He asks for magic items to get us home.
- Alayah busts out the magic beans. They become ten magic toads.
- The toads are magic. We touch one. It becomes a genie. Bogsū accidentally immediately gives it to the strange guy.
- Alayah decides to touch all the toads at once. One becomes a balor. The next becomes a sahuagin. The next becomes a grimlock. Next an adult silver dragon. Then a merfolk. Then a pegasus. A xorn. A giant rat. Another xorn. A cloud giant.
- We beg the shopkeeper to help us. He uses the genie to get rid of all the monsters. He sends us home. Ahmed not with us, he was crushed.
- We are suddenly back out in the centre of the maze. Back in the prime.
- There is magic below us.
- There is text when we dig. We read about the Second Cataclysm and the God-Felling Star. A history of when Taiia split herself into six champions to fight the GFS, or six mortals came together to fight. Less well documented than the Demon War. Also caused St Malia to split from the mainland.
- We gather up the paper and the marble stone with the story written on it. Turns out to be an altar. Has a figure wearing a cloak of feathers.
- Faezel is the GFS and the author?
- The marble altar is full of cracks. We drop in a pebble with Light cast on it. Eliyah becomes an ant and crawls in. Sees another altar inside, with more figures inside.
- Suddenly something grabs Eliyah's ant antennae. Blue human hands! Eliyah healed to full health somehow.
- Voice says help.
- Guy with hand outstretched was on the internal altar.
- Bogsū tries to get a vibe from the altar. Hears voices saying "Help us" and another responding "No."
- We decide to return to the church and the Deacon.
- Outer: Saint Carafect.
- Saint Corellon and Saint Rillifane are the two trapped on the inside altar.
- There were six saints. There were six monarchs.
- We plan to return there tomorrow with the Deacon. We ask the army commander to put a few soldiers together to come with us.
- Army Commander Ject is going with us with five soldiers.
- The altar is still there. Very weird but we learn nothing. No clue but the Deacon is perturbed. We leave the maze again.
