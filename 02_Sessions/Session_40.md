## Summary

- The party leaves the fire giant town and emerges outside into cold.
- They arrive at Gufragel, the frost giant town, and go to the Eynhall.
- Jarl Stoor gives them a cold reception but enjoys the rivalry between the party and Karl's crew, who arrived by elevator.
- From Gufragel, the party can see across Ymleif as far as Grima.
- Jarl Stoor reports lost scouts around the mountain, possibly due to yeti.
- Eliyah scries on the scouts using giant cloth and sees corpses, blood and footprints that do not leave the area.
- Bogsū buys a cloth that gives +1 AC when he is above 75% health.
- At the site of the dead scouts, the party experiences hallucinations and retreats to a stone shack.
- The party fights a Megurash on the roof, with Bogsū badly hurt and then polymorphed into a giant ape.

## Source Notes

- We leave the fire giant town. Through the tunnels to an exit and we're suddenly outside and cold again.
- Frost giant town. Built-up area ahead. Frost giants lumbering around.
- Gufragel is the town. To the Eynhall.
- The elders eye us up. Cold reception.
- We stare out over the continent of Ymleif, can see all the way to Grima.
- Frost giant king guy has lighter skin, tattoos, dreads. Jarl Stoor.
- Fucking Karl and co are here! Took the elevator.
- We argue with them about who gets to go to the top of the Spire.
- Jarl reckons our rivalry has the makings of a classic tapestry saga.
- Jarl tells us some scouts have been lost around the mountain. Possibly yeti.
- Elia goes scrying with giant cloth. Frost giant scouts. On the mountain pass. Pools of blood. Footprints, not giant. Not leaving the area.
- The cloth giant lady sells us some cloth. Bogsū buys one that gives +1 AC when above 75% health.
- Find the corpses at the spot Elia scried. Suddenly shit gets weird. Bryce hallucinates something. We stand back to back. Make our way to a little shack.
- Big awful fight with Megurash. We light up the shack but the fight happens on the stone roof. Bogsū hit hard. Elia polymorphs him into a giant ape.
