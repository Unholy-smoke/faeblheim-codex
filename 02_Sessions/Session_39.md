## Summary

- The party helps clean up the undead creatures around Levragel.
- A roc flies overhead; the party attacks it and the fire giants finish it off.
- Chieftain Enora, leader of the fire giants, is aware of the Megurash but surprised by the dead creatures awakening.
- Enora's cleric unpetrifies Eliyah, and the party is given a human-sized guest lodge.
- A fire giant water-bearer melts Bogsū's gold piece in his hand and pours it out as a bracelet for him.
- At Enora's banquet, the food withers on the plate and the party is attacked.
- Enora runs down the corridor and disappears.
- The party fights and kills a crawling undead creature that summons skeletons.
- Bogsū tries to persuade Enora while on 4 hp; she tells him to climb the Spire, defeat the ancient evil, and catch her on the way down.
