### Overview

Reality-tuning founder living in a pocket universe; hates "inauthentic" glitches more than players and wants to cleanse Faeblheim of players.

**Aliases:** Adonis Blue, Dagger.

**First seen:** Session 10; **Last seen:** Session 43.

### Notable moments

- **S10:** Mrs Haingrove wants the dagger Isthmia told the party about.
- **S23:** A strange man appears while the party rests: possibly a game founder or developer, Adonis Blue, "Dagger".
- **S31:** Dagger appears in response to glitched items, deletes them, and reacts angrily to mention of the outside world. He wants to reset the game free of players, but suggests he might be convinced by a commitment to authenticity and honesty in-game.
- **S32:** Alayah finds Adonis Blue in his pocket universe and convinces him to hide himself with a glamour, causing the game to blur the quest for her.
- **S43:** Dagger is revealed as the mastermind behind the altar at the top of the [[Worldspire]] that was draining [[Taiia]]'s power. He raises the [[Ceran_Palace]] a hundred feet into the air on a stone platform like another Worldspire, and a freed Saint identifies him as one of the [[Monarchs]].
