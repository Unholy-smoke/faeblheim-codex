Fire giant chieftain of [[Levragel]]. She knew of the [[Megurash]] but was surprised when dead creatures around the citadel reanimated. Her cleric unpetrified Eliyah. After Bogsū failed to persuade her romantically, she took his potion of growth and told him to climb the Spire, defeat the ancient evil, and catch her on the way down.

**First seen:** Session 39; **Last seen:** Session 39.
