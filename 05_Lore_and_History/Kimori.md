Yukai Kimori is named as one of the founders connected to Faeblheim, alongside [[Hiki]]. [[Karl's Diary|Karl's Diary]] records speculation that Hiki, Kimori, or even the [[Monarchs]] may have sent forces into the game to clear it out.
