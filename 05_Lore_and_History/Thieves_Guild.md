### Overview
Grima underworld institution with a practical relationship to the party. Bryce has ties to the guild, the party sells them a stolen book early on, and Bryce later has access to a Thieves Guild apartment.

**First seen:** Session 01; **Last seen:** Session 26.

### Notable mentions
- **S01:** The party is thrown out of the Thieves Guild for overreaching.
- **S04:** The party delivers a book to the guild and is paid 25 platinum.
- **S26:** The party sleeps at Bryce's Thieves Guild apartment.
