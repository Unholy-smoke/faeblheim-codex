### Overview
Early elite testers or players connected to the foundations of Faeblheim. They overlap conceptually with the [[Monarchs]] and the founders, though the exact boundaries between alpha testers, founders and Monarchs remain unclear.

**First seen:** Session 17; **Last seen:** Session 24.

### Notes
- Session 17 references five players who started the game with founders Hiki and Kimori.
- Later lore increasingly points toward a powerful early group whose members may still shape reality inside Faeblheim.
