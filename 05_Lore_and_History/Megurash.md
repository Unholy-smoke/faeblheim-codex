Evil spirits from long ago, recognised by [[Emory]] after the party experienced visions of a staring face. They are threatening the giants again and appear connected to the murders across the [[Worldspire]].

Known signs include ghostly figures that appear and vanish, psychic attacks, hallucinations, corpses with throat slashes and chest or back wounds, and spirits such as [[Midnakesh]].

**First seen:** Session 36; **Last seen:** Session 41.
