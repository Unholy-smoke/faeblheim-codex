### Overview
Political and mechanical fault line around Faeblheim's strict ban on player-versus-player killing. The ban shapes the rise of the [[Player_Killers|Player Killers]], the moral conflict around Karl's crew, and the broader fear that someone might remove the safety rails to empty or reset the game.

**First seen:** Session 01; **Last seen:** Session 24.
