### Overview

A group of powerful early players or developers known as Monarchs. Names include Adonis Blue, Holy Blue, Grayling, Argus Scatch, and an unnamed fifth. They are tied closely to the foundational development of Faeblheim.

**Aliases:** The Monarchs.

**First seen:** Session —; **Last seen:** Session 43.

### Session 43

A freed Saint states that he is one of the Monarchs, and that [[Adonis_Blue_(Dagger)|Adonis Blue (Dagger)]] is one too. This creates a new link between the Monarchs, the [[Six_Saints|Six Saints]], and the struggle against Dagger.
