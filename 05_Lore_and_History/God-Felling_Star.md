The God-Felling Star was the Second Cataclysm, prevented through the intervention of the [[Six_Saints|Six Saints]]. The party found writings beneath the palace maze describing Taiia splitting herself into six champions, or six mortals coming together, to fight the Star.

The notes also raise the possibility that Faezel is connected to the God-Felling Star and may be the author of the recovered text.

**First seen:** Session 33; **Last seen:** Session 33.
